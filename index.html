What is it

▼
    Released in February 25, 2022 directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki popular for his souls series. Elden Ring is a game that belong to the genre of dark souls. The game combines Dark fantasy, RPG (role playing game), and open world type aspects. The whole premise of the game is you a tarnished must go aorund the world/map and restore order and become Elden Lord. Because of its popularity during the time it quickly became the Game of the Year in 2022
