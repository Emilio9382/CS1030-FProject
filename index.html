Welcome to Elden Ring

What is it

▼
    Released in February 25, 2022 directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki popular for his souls series. Elden Ring is a game that belong to the genre of dark souls. The game combines Dark fantasy, RPG (role playing game), and open world type aspects. The whole premise of the game is you a tarnished must go aorund the world/map and restore order and become Elden Lord. Because of its popularity during the time it quickly became the Game of the Year in 2022

How to play

▼
    Exploration is a big part of this game. You go around to the different regions unlocking the grace which is basically like a waypoint you can teleport to. You go around defeating enemies that in turn give you currency that you can use to level up your character/worrier/mage. While you explore the map you'll come across dungeons where you'll be able to obtain treasures like armour or weapons or craftable parts/weapon upgrades. Some weapons can be found just by exploring. After you deem yourself worthy you'll go ahead and defeat one of main bosses and collect his rememberance to unlock endgame content. The thing with these type of games is you'll end up dying more times than you can count. Whcih is why upgrading your character wether weapons or level can be really useful.
